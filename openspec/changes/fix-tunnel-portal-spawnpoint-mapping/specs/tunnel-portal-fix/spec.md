## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Tunnel portal click selects matching station

Clicking a tunnel portal on the map SHALL open the planning panel with the station that matches the portal's label selected.

#### Scenario: Click west tunnel portal
- **WHEN** the player clicks the left tunnel portal (labeled "West Station")
- **THEN** the planning panel opens with West Station selected and West Station's inventory displayed

#### Scenario: Click east tunnel portal
- **WHEN** the player clicks the right tunnel portal (labeled "East Station")
- **THEN** the planning panel opens with East Station selected and East Station's inventory displayed
